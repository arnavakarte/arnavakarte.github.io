Intro

Hello and welcome to my portfolio website. My name is Arnav, and I am a recent graduate from Loyola Marymount University in Los Angeles. I completed a Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Business Administration.

You may be wondering why I chose this academic combination. It's because as much as I love all aspects of engineering, I crave knowledge. Ever since I was little, I've always wanted to learn as much as I can about anything I can. I am passionate about diversifying my interests and discovering new things.
